Signs it might be time for a new roof

Not sure if it's time?
Watch for these.

If you spot any of these, book a free estimate — catching it early can save you thousands.

  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Granules collecting in your gutters
  • Water stains on ceilings or in the attic
  • Daylight visible through the roof boards
  • Sagging rooflines or soft spots
  • Your roof is 15–20+ years old
  • Recent storm, heavy rain or high winds
  • Rising energy bills from poor insulation
